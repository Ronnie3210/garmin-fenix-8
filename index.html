How to Install the Garmin Fenix 8 Update

  1. Using Garmin Connect Mobile: Pair your watch with your smartphone and open Garmin Connect. The update will download automatically when available.
  2. Using Garmin Express: Connect your Fenix 8 to your computer via USB and open Garmin Express. Select your device and install the available update.
  3. Ensure your watch has at least 50% battery before starting.
  4. Follow on-screen prompts and let the device restart once complete.

Troubleshooting Update Issues

  • Update not downloading? Check Wi-Fi or USB connection and try again.
  • Insufficient storage? Remove older activities or apps to free up space.
  • Battery draining quickly? Restart the watch and disable unused sensors.
  • Still stuck? Contact Garmin Support for further assistance.
